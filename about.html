About Spraymaster Ltd.

Spraymaster Ltd. has been the premier auto collision repair specialist and trusted BMW and other top automotive brands spare parts stockist in Nairobi, Kenya, for the past 25 years. We take pride in our expertise, precision, and customer-focused approach in every repair and restoration project.

Repairing and restoring today’s high-tech vehicles requires patience, precision, and extensive knowledge of sophisticated equipment. Our team of professionals, advanced technology, and years of experience ensure that your vehicle is repaired correctly the first time. Our goal is perfection and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide quality accident repair services efficiently with integrity and excellent customer service.

Vision: To be the premier automotive body shop in East Africa.

Our Values

  • Accountability – We are accountable to our clients.
  • Integrity – We are honest, fair, and respectful of others.
  • Employees – We value and recognize employee contributions and talent.
  • Efficiency – We identify the best ways to deliver high-quality services using our resources efficiently.
  • Effectiveness – We make informed decisions that make a difference.
  • Innovation – We value new ideas and concepts.
