Our Spray Booth

State-of-the-Art Dust-Free Spray Booth

At Spraymaster Ltd., our spray booth is designed to deliver perfect paint finishes every time. Our dust-free, climate-controlled booth ensures a safe and clean environment for all painting and refinishing tasks.

Equipped with advanced Max Meyer paint matching and mixing technology, our spray booth guarantees precise color replication and smooth, flawless finishes for every vehicle. Safety, efficiency, and quality are our top priorities.

Features of Our Spray Booth

Our spray booth combines technology and expertise to provide optimal painting results.

  • Dust-free and climate-controlled environment
  • Advanced Max Meyer paint matching system
  • Safe and compliant with industry standards
  • Efficient workflow for faster turnaround
  • Optimal lighting for precision painting
  • Flawless, high-quality paint finishes
